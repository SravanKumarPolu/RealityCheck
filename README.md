# RealityCheck - Decision & Regret Trainer

**Tagline:** "Predict your choices. Train your future."

## Overview

RealityCheck is an innovative Android app designed to help users improve their decision-making skills by tracking predictions, recording outcomes, and analyzing accuracy patterns. It's not just a journal‚Äîit's a "decision gym" where you train your intuition.

## Why RealityCheck is Different

### The Problem with Most Decision/Journal Apps

Most "decision" or "journal" apps in the market have a fundamental flaw:

‚ùå **They let you log what happened, not what you thought would happen**  
‚ùå **They don't calculate prediction vs. reality error**  
‚ùå **They don't give you a skill-like score (Decision Accuracy) or Regret Index**  
‚ùå **They focus on generic wellness tracking, not decision training**

These apps are **backward-looking diaries** that let you reflect on past events, but they don't help you **improve your prediction skills** for future decisions.

### Our Uniqueness: Training Your Decision Muscle

RealityCheck is fundamentally different because:

‚úÖ **Prediction-First Approach**: You log what you **think will happen** BEFORE acting, then compare it to reality  
‚úÖ **Quantitative Error Calculation**: Automatically calculates the difference between your predictions and actual outcomes  
‚úÖ **Skill-Based Scoring**: Gives you a **Decision Accuracy Score (0-100%)** and **Regret Index** like a fitness tracker for your intuition  
‚úÖ **Decision Training Focus**: It's a "decision gym" where you train your intuition, not just track mood or habits  
‚úÖ **Miscalibration Detection**: Identifies where you're overconfident or underestimate impacts

**Core Philosophy**: *Most apps help you understand the past. RealityCheck helps you predict the future better.*
